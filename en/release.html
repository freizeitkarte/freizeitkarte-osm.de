Underlying data:
The Freizeitkarte maps are based on OpenStreetMap data of the March 23th, 2014 .
Thanks to all the mappers who contributed data.

Additions / modifications in Release 14.03:

  • general improvement of the map data (by the OSM project)
  • general improvement of the map representation for:
    • stations and substations
    • hedges and fences
    • public toilets
  • maps for the Pyrenees and Carpathians
  • Windowsinstaller also in english

Next release:
The next release is planned for mid of May 2014.
