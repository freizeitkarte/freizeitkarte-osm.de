Underlying data:
The Freizeitkarte Project maps are based on OpenStreetMap data of the Novemver 10th, 2013.
Thanks to all the mappers who contributed data.

Additions / modifications in Release 13.11:

  • general improvement of the map data (by the OSM project)
  • general improvement of the map representation
    • representation of monorails
    • correct representation of streets tagged with "area=no"
    • the borders of national parks are first shown on a higher zoom level
    • agricultural, country or dirt roads (quality grades 4,5) are dismissed later
    • smaller icon for radio masts
  • general improvement of the address search

Next release:
The next release of The Freizeitkarte Project maps is planned probably at mid of January 2014.
