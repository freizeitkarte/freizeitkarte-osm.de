Underlying data:
The Freizeitkarte maps are based on OpenStreetMap data of the January 19 th, 2014 .
Thanks to all the mappers who contributed data.

Additions / modifications in Release 14.01:

  • general improvement of the map data (by the OSM project)
  • general improvement of the map representation for:
    • marshland
    • mudflats
    • marinas
  • text error fix

Next release:
The next release is planned for mid of March 2014.

Current maps:
Downloads of the maps are divided into regions or countries. You can find the links (e.g. Germany, Austria, Switzerland, ...) in the left navigation. Advanced users can use this link to the maps directory.
